Common WordPress FAQ

You're probably wondering...

Web design agencies are all over the map—on price, timeline, and offer. Why?

Because every agency operates under different constraints:
team quality, location, approach, and cost of labor.

So let’s cut through the noise and show you exactly where we stand.

Why should I choose WordPress for my website?

WordPress powers over 40% of the internet for a reason. It’s flexible, scalable, and endlessly customizable—whether you need a sleek marketing site, a robust eCommerce store, or something totally custom. Plus, you actually own it (no being stuck in a closed platform).

Are WordPress websites hard to manage?

Not the way we build it. We create a custom admin experience that’s clean, intuitive, and visual, so your team can make updates in minutes without touching a single line of code. If you can edit a doc, you can run your site.

Can PumaPulse build a completely custom WordPress site?

Absolutely. Templates are fine for quick DIY projects, but we specialize in custom WordPress development.

Every site we build is fully custom with no cookie-cutter templates and no bloated themes. You get a fast, flexible site that looks and feels like you, not your competitors.

Will my WordPress site be fast and secure?

Yes, and yes. We bake performance and security into every build—optimized code, smart hosting recommendations, caching, and the right security plugins. We use hardened security protocols, WordFence, and scalable infrastructure to make sure your site is fast, safe, and built to grow with your business.

A fast, secure site isn’t an add-on for us, it’s the baseline.

How much does a custom WordPress site cost?

Like any custom project, pricing depends on complexity, features, and scale, but typically, our WordPress websites start at $50k. That said, we’ll scope things out with you upfront so there are no surprises. Our focus is always on building a site that delivers ROI, not just a pretty design.

Can you maintain and update my WordPress site after launch?

Definitely. Think of us as your long-term web partner, not just the launch crew. We offer ongoing maintenance, support, and optimizations to keep your site running smoothly and evolving with your business.
