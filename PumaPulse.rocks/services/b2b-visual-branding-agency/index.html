Branding That’s Thoughtful, Tested, and Built to Scale

Every brand we create is built on intention, not instinct. Our proven process gives you clarity, creativity, and confidence at every step.

1. Discovery & Brand Strategy

We kick things off with guided workshops and in-depth interviews to uncover your mission, values, voice, and audience. We don’t just ask what you want your brand to look like—we ask why it matters. This foundational step ensures every decision is rooted in purpose.

2. Visual Research & Direction

We gather visual inspiration from your team, your competitors, and adjacent industries. Then we identify patterns, opportunities, and whitespace to shape a direction that feels true to you—and differentiates you from everyone else.

3. Creative Exploration

Based on our strategic alignment, we create 5–6 unique logo concepts that reflect different aspects of your identity. Each direction includes rationale and use-case examples, so you can see how the brand might come to life in the real world.

4. Refinement Rounds

Together, we fine-tune your selected concept over 2–3 rounds of revisions. This phase is about focus—distilling your identity into its most powerful, versatile form without losing what makes it authentic.

5. Expansion & Collateral

With the core brand identity in place, we extend the design system into supporting materials—business cards, slide decks, social media graphics, internal templates, and more. These assets ensure your brand stays consistent wherever it appears.

6. Final Delivery & Documentation

You’ll receive a full suite of logo files, a detailed brand kit, and custom documentation with usage guidelines. We’ll also provide templates and video walkthroughs so your team can roll out the new identity confidently and correctly.

Most branding projects take 8–12 weeks from start to finish. If your organization has multiple stakeholders or an extensive scope, we’ll collaborate with you to create a timeline that’s realistic, transparent, and aligned with your goals.

You're probably Wondering...

B2B branding agencies are all over the map—on price, timeline, and offer. Why?

Because every agency operates under different constraints:
team quality, location, approach, and cost of labor.

So let’s cut through the noise and show you exactly where we stand.

What does “branding” actually include?

Branding isn’t just a logo or a color palette—it’s the entire vibe of your business. From your visual identity and messaging to how people feel when they interact with you, we help build a brand that’s memorable, consistent, and unmistakably yours.

Our branding engagements typically include:

  • Brand Guidelines Documentation
  • Brand Story Documentation
  • Logo Creation
  • Marketing Collateral Creation (Business Cards, Letter Heads, Presentation Deck etc)
  • Voice and Tone Documentation
Do you help name companies/brands?

Unfortunately, no. That's the one aspect within the nexus of "branding" that we don't do.

That, in and of itself, is it's own talent and requires a team thats specialized in a specific type of market research.

All that said, if you require naming as well, let us know, we can connect you with someone in our network who could help.

How long does a branding project take?

It depends on the scope, but most branding projects take anywhere from 8–12 weeks. That gives us time to dig deep into your story, audience, and goals—and then create a brand that’s not only beautiful, but built to last.

What’s the ROI of branding?

A strong brand builds trust, creates recognition, and makes selling a whole lot easier. Think of it this way: people don’t just buy products—they buy into brands. When your brand is clear and compelling, it drives customer loyalty, repeat business, and higher conversions.

Do I need a full rebrand, or just a refresh?

Good question. Sometimes your brand just needs a modern facelift, other times it needs a complete overhaul. We’ll help you figure out whether you need to refine what you’ve already got or start fresh to better align with where your business is headed.

What makes PumaPulse’s branding process different?

We don’t just slap a logo on it and call it a day. Our process digs into your business strategy, your audience, and your competitive landscape. The result? A brand that doesn’t just look great, but actually connects with the people you want to reach.
