AI Website Redesign FAQs

You're probably wondering...

Web design agencies are all over the map—on price, timeline, and offer. Why?

Because every agency operates under different constraints:
team quality, location, approach, and cost of labor.

So let’s cut through the noise and show you exactly where we stand.

What’s the price range for an AI website design project?

Most AI website projects land between $50,000 and $100,000. You’re not just buying design. You’re investing in clarity, credibility, and conversions. We help you simplify the complex, showcase your tech, and position your brand as a trusted leader in a space where trust is everything.

How long does an AI website project typically take?

On average, 12–16 weeks. That includes deep discovery, messaging, design, custom development, and testing. We understand the pace of innovation in AI, so we build fast, with future updates and scalability in mind.

Can you help explain our AI capabilities to non-technical users?

Absolutely. One of our superpowers is translating advanced concepts into simple, compelling storytelling. Whether it’s machine learning, NLP, or predictive analytics, we’ll help you communicate the value in a way that clicks with your target audience.

Do you integrate live demos or AI-powered tools on the site?

Yes. From interactive product demos to real-time AI assistants and chatbot integrations, we can build functionality that lets your product do the talking. We’ll make sure it’s seamless, secure, and aligned with your conversion goals.
