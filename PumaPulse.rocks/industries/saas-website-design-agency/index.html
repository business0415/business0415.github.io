SaaS Website Design FAQs

You're probably wondering...

Web design agencies are all over the map—on price, timeline, and offer. Why?

Because every agency operates under different constraints:
team quality, location, approach, and cost of labor.

So let’s cut through the noise and show you exactly where we stand.

How much does a SaaS website design project usually cost?

Most of our SaaS website redesigns fall between $50,000 and $100,000. Why the range? Because every product, audience, and growth goal is different. We don’t just slap your brand on a template. We dig deep, strategize around conversions, and custom-build every page to support your sales funnel. The result? A site that pays for itself fast.

What’s the timeline for a SaaS website redesign or launch?

Expect a 12–16 week process from kickoff to go-live. That includes research, messaging, wireframing, custom design, dev, QA—you name it. We move quickly, but never at the expense of strategy. Our goal is to launch a website that not only looks sharp, but accelerates your sales cycle and scales with your product.

Do you help with landing pages and conversion optimization?

100%. Your website isn’t just a digital brochure. It’s a growth engine. We design high-converting landing pages, run A/B tests, and continuously optimize for better performance. Whether you're running paid campaigns, rolling out a new feature, or nurturing leads, we’ll help turn clicks into customers.

What makes a PumaPulse SaaS website different from our competitors?

We get SaaS and it shows. Our websites don’t just explain your product; they sell it. We blend sharp messaging, intuitive UX, and conversion-focused design to highlight your value prop and drive demos. Most importantly, we build sites that grow with you, modular, easy to update, and always performance-first.

Bottom line? Your competitors’ websites talk. Yours will convert.
