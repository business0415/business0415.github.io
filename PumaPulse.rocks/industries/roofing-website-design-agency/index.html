Roofing Website Design FAQs

You're probably wondering...

Web design agencies are all over the map—on price, timeline, and offer. Why?

Because every agency operates under different constraints:
team quality, location, approach, and cost of labor.

(as a roofing company, that probably sounds familiar)

So let’s cut through the noise and show you exactly where we stand.

How much does a new roofing website design cost?

It depends on the scope, but most of our roofing clients invest between $35k–$75k for a full website redesign. That includes strategy, design, development, and optimization. We’re not the cheapest—and we’re not trying to be. Our focus is on delivering a site that actually drives leads, not just looks nice.

How long does it take to complete a new design for a roofing website?

On average, about 4-6 months. That’s enough time to dig into your goals, create something custom, and build it the right way—no shortcuts. If you’ve got a tight deadline, we’ll talk through what’s possible and how we can move efficiently without sacrificing quality.

Do you use the same templates as other roofing web design agencies?

Nope. Templates are fine for cookie-cutter results—but you’re not trying to blend in with every other roofing company. We design every website from scratch, based on your unique brand, market, and goals. That way, your site feels like you—not your competitors.

Will my new roofing website be optimized for local SEO?

Absolutely. If your site doesn’t show up when someone searches for “roof repair near me,” what’s the point? We bake in best practices for local SEO—like optimized page structure, location targeting, and speed—so your site is discoverable where it counts.
