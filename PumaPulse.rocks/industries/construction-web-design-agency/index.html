Construction Company Website FAQ

You're probably wondering...

Web design agencies are all over the map—on price, timeline, and offer. Why?

Because every agency operates under different constraints:
team quality, location, approach, and cost of labor.

(as a construction company, that probably sounds familiar)

So let’s cut through the noise and show you exactly where we stand.

Why choose PumaPulse for your custom construction website design?

Because your work deserves more than a templated site and a one-size-fits-all agency.

At PumaPulse, we specialize in building websites that communicate credibility, showcase your capabilities, and position you as the clear industry leader. Construction companies come to us when they need a site that:

  • Shows off their projects in visually compelling ways
  • Clarifies exactly what they do (and where they do it)
  • Helps them attract top talent
  • Feels easy to update and professional enough for a C-suite presentation

With deep experience working with firms like Skyline and HITT, we know what it takes to build a website that makes your team, your work, and your reputation stand tall.

What type of construction companies do you build websites for?

We work with construction companies that are serious about their craft and ready to show it. That includes:

  • Commercial Contractors – General contractors, design-build firms, and civil engineering companies
  • Residential Builders – Custom home builders and high-end remodelers
  • Specialty Contractors – HVAC, electrical, plumbing, and other trades
  • Public Sector & Infrastructure Firms – Especially those who regularly submit bids or work across multiple jurisdictions

Whether you're showcasing large-scale developments or intricate, high-end work, our goal is the same: Build a site that makes it unmistakably clear what you do—and why you’re the right team to do it.

How long does it typically take to design a construction website?

Most construction website projects take between 4-6 months from kickoff to launch.

This timeline includes brand discovery, content strategy, custom design, development, and QA. We move fast, but never at the expense of getting it right. You’ll be involved in key milestones, but we take the heavy lifting off your plate so your team can stay focused on the jobsite (not your website).

What elements are essential for the best construction company website?

The best construction websites don’t just look good—they work for you. That means:

  • Clear, scannable service overviews
  • Robust project galleries that highlight your capabilities
  • Interactive maps showing service areas or project locations
  • Dynamic team and career pages to support recruiting
  • Easy-to-use backend so your team can update content without headaches

Most importantly, your website should reflect the level of professionalism and pride you bring to your real-world work.

Is there a difference between residential and commercial construction website designs?

Absolutely—and it’s not just visual.

Commercial construction websites focus on scale, partnerships, and project types. They need to communicate trust, compliance, and capability to win RFPs and impress stakeholders.

Residential construction websites lean more into emotion, lifestyle, and design. Homeowners want to see themselves in your work and feel inspired.

We tailor every website to the needs of your audience and industry niche—because the right message and layout can make all the difference.

How much does it cost to design a construction company website?

Most construction websites we build fall in the range of $50,000–$100,000, depending on size, complexity, and functionality.

This isn’t just a cost—it’s an investment in how your company is perceived and how effectively you can scale. We’ve seen clients use their new website to:

  • Win multi-million dollar projects
  • Attract top-tier talent
  • Consolidate their brand after acquisitions
  • Position themselves for the next phase of growth
