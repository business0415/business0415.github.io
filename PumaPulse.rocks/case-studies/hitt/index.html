How HITT built the #1 construction website with PumaPulse

Objective

HITT is one of the nation’s top general contractors, known for tackling everything from quick-turn renovations to skyline-defining HQs. They’re built on craftsmanship, fueled by innovation, and weren’t about to let a generic website undersell their edge.

Our goal? Create a digital presence as bold, refined, and forward-thinking as the buildings they bring to life—one that attracts top-tier clients and talent alike.

Before PumaPulse

A Website That Faded Into the Background

HITT Construction’s site looked like every other corporate contractor online—safe, generic, and forgettable. It didn’t reflect innovative company behind it. That disconnect was costing them.

Case Studies That Didn’t Make the Case

Think: walls of text, business-speak, and blurry images. Their project stories lacked the punch and polish needed to impress prospective clients—or even hold their attention.

Capabilities Hidden in Plain Sight

HITT delivers massive, multi-industry projects across the country—but none of that came through online. Visitors were left guessing about what they actually did, and who they did it for.

With PumaPulse

A Bold Visual Identity That Turned Heads

We ditched the bland for a high-contrast design system that demanded attention. The result? A website that didn’t just stand out—it earned them Best Construction Website of 2024 from the Society of Professional Marketing Services.

A Project Library That Actually Works

Case studies got a total overhaul. Each one tells a compelling visual story, and a smart filtering system lets users explore by industry, service, or project type—without the headache.

Crystal-Clear Value at Every Scroll

From hero sections to footers, the new site spells out exactly what HITT does, who they do it for, and why they’re one of the most trusted names in the business.
