How HySafe’s New Website Delivered a 217% Lead Increase

Objective

HySafe builds custom fall protection systems for industries like construction, manufacturing, and transportation. These solutions are engineered to fit each environment, but their old website didn’t reflect that level of precision. Key product details were hard to find, the mobile experience was frustrating, and the quote request process lacked clarity, creating friction where there should have been momentum.

They needed a site that did what their systems do: protect and guide. One that clearly explained what they offer, showcased their work in the field, and made it easy to request a quote on any device, with zero confusion. That’s where we came in.

Before PumaPulse

Not Enough Clarity, Not Enough Confidence

The old site didn’t give visitors the info they needed to feel confident. It glossed over key details, didn’t explain how HySafe’s solutions worked in real job sites, and failed to show what made them different.

A Navigation Experience That Slowed Everything Down

Finding the right product was a chore. Important specs and part numbers were buried, and the path to requesting a quote was anything but straightforward. Every extra click was a missed opportunity.

Mobile That Didn't Move With the User

On smaller screens, the site lost its footing. Navigation was clunky, images broke the layout, and the overall experience made it tough to engage, especially for users in the field.

With PumaPulse

A Visual System That Finally Matches the Brand

We created a clean, cohesive design grounded in HySafe’s mission. Sky-toned backgrounds, custom icons, and real project imagery give the site a professional polish, while subtle animation adds life without overwhelming the user.

Content That Guides, Not Confuses

The content strategy was rebuilt from the ground up. Product filters, dropdown modules, and clearly labeled specs make it easy to explore. And with quote requests just a click away, there's zero confusion about what to do next.

Mobile That Works Wherever the Work Happens

The entire experience is now fully responsive and user-friendly across every device. Interactive elements like sliders and tooltips guide users through the process, while case studies show HySafe’s work in action, instantly building trust.

It was refreshing that there was no ego or attitude when we asked questions or wanted to take a different approach.”
