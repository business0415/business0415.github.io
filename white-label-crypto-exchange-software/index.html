Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is a white-label crypto exchange?

A white-label crypto exchange is a ready-made platform that allows you to quickly start your own cryptocurrency exchange. It's already built for you, but you can customize it by adding your brand name, logo, and other features to make it your own. This option saves you both time and money because you don’t have to build the exchange from the ground up..

Q2. How much does a white-label crypto exchange cost?

The cost of a white-label crypto exchange can vary based on what you need, even though it is pre-built. It is determined by your specifications, features, and desired customizations. On average, it can cost anywhere from $20,000 to $50,000 or more. This is generally more affordable than building a crypto exchange from scratch. At Pumapulse, we offer competitive pricing to help you get started with your cryptocurrency exchange business at the best value.

Q3. How long does it take to launch a white-label crypto exchange?

Launching a white-label crypto exchange usually takes about 4 to 6 weeks. But the exact time can change depending on how much customization you want and the features you need. The more you want to personalize or add, the longer it might take.

Q4. Is a white-label crypto exchange secure?

Yes, white-label crypto exchanges are built to be secure. Trusted providers offer security features like two-factor authentication, encryption, DDoS protection, and safe wallet connections. But it's important to pick a provider, like Pumapulse, with a proven history of strong security. Always make sure you're choosing one with a good track record!

Q5. What are the key features of a white-label crypto exchange?

Here are some key features of a white-label crypto exchange:

  • Easy-to-Use Interface – The platform should be simple, so users can trade easily without confusion.
  • Support for Multiple Cryptocurrencies – The exchange should allow trading in various cryptocurrencies, so users have more options.
  • High Liquidity – This ensures that users can buy and sell crypto quickly, without delays or price slippage.
  • Secure Wallets – Protect your users' funds with secure wallets, keeping their cryptocurrencies safe.
  • KYC/AML Compliance – This is important for following legal requirements and ensuring that your platform operates within the law.
  • Fast Trading Engine – A good trading engine matches buy and sell orders quickly, providing a smooth experience.

These features can be customized to suit your business needs, ensuring you offer the best experience for your users.
