Why Start a Neobank App Today?

Nowadays, people prefer to manage their money through apps instead of visiting physical branches. The neobank app exactly offers this. In 2025, Neobank is on the way to reaching $4 trillion with a CAGR of 54.8%. 

Here are some other reasons why starting a neobank app today makes sense:

  • Most people, especially Gen Z, prefer banking on their phones.
  • New tech and cloud tools make launching a neobank faster and affordable.
  • Freelancers, SMEs, and rural users need better digital banking.
  • Neobanks can update and grow faster than traditional banks.
  • Fintech is growing fast, and smart neobanks attract investors easily.

In simple terms, the market is growing, the demand is real, and the tools are ready. This is the perfect time to launch a neobank app and provide the easiest way of digital banking.

How Much Will It Cost to Build a Neobank App in 2025 and 2026?

In 2025, the cost to build a neobank app is usually between $50,000 and $300,000. The exact price depends on design, features, team size, and platform.

For 2026, we can expect this cost to rise. A neobank app may range from $60,000 to $400,000, since new technologies, security needs, and advanced features will play a bigger role.

So, in short:

  • 2025 cost → $50,000 to $300,000
     
  • 2026 expected cost → $60,000 to $400,000

UI/UX Design
A simple and clean UI/UX design helps in retaining users. A basic design may cost $10,000 to $30,000, while a custom design with animations and advanced features can go above $50,000. Smooth navigation and easy use directly improve user satisfaction.

Features
The more features you add, the higher the cost. Basic features include sign-up, balance checking, and transactions. Advanced options like payment gateway integration, biometric login, analytics, or real-time insights can push costs between $50,000 and $300,000, based on complexity.

Platform Choice
Choosing between native or cross-platform development also matters. Native apps for iOS and Android cost more, around $50,000 to $200,000. Cross-platform apps are cheaper, usually $75,000 to $150,000, and work across devices.

App Complexity
A basic app with simple functions may cost $50,000 to $100,000. But if you add AI insights, blockchain support, fraud detection, or multi-currency features, costs can go up to $250,000 or more.

Technology Stack
Your tech stack affects both speed and price. Using React Native or Kotlin is budget-friendly. But if you go for advanced tech like blockchain or AI, costs may rise by $50,000 to $100,000 more.

Development Location
Costs vary based on where the company is located. In the US or Western Europe, charges are $100 to $250 per hour. In India, Asia, or Eastern Europe, it is more affordable, around $30 to $75 per hour.

Team Size
A bigger team means faster delivery but also higher cost. A skilled team with developers, designers, and testers may cost $100,000 to $250,000, depending on project needs.

Maintenance Costs
Even after launch, you need to maintain the app. Regular updates, bug fixes, and security patches cost about 15-20% of the app cost every year. For a $100,000 app, this will be around $15,000 to $20,000 annually.

Cost-Saving Tips for Developing a Neobank App

You don’t need a massive budget to build a neobank app. With the right approach, you can use a minimal amount while still providing a high-quality, secure, and user-friendly solution. Let’s look into some smart tips to save money:

✅ Build a Simple MVP First

Start small with an MVP (Minimum Viable Product). Focus on the key features, such as onboarding, KYC, wallet, and transactions. This helps you to launch quickly, test your idea, and avoid wasting money on features that users may not need now.

✅ Work with a Fintech-Experienced Team

Choose a development partner who understands the fintech industry. They handle security, compliance, and properly create a neobank app, which saves you time, rework, and legal issues.

👉 Need a fintech-focused dev team? Contact us

✅ Use Ready-Made APIs and Tools

While developing a neobank, use reliable third-party services for KYC, payments, and fraud detection to speed up development and reduce costs. You can also use custom-built features, which are secure and fast to integrate.

✅ Keep the Design Clean and User-Friendly

You don’t need flashy animations or complex designs in your neobank app. Go for a simple, clean interface that users can navigate easily. It saves design time, cost, and gives users a better experience.

✅ Choose Cloud Hosting

Apply cloud platforms like AWS or Google Cloud. They’re affordable, customizable, and offer a startup-friendly option. You’ll only pay for what you use, and you don’t need to handle servers.

✅ Avoid Shortcuts That Cause Trouble Later

Low-cost code or quick fixes may save money at first, but they will cost more in the long term. Create a strong foundation for your project by starting with clean, modular code.

✅ Plan for Support and Updates

After your Neobank app goes live, you’ll still need to fix bugs, add updates, and keep it secure. Make sure to plan for these costs early, so there are no surprises later.

By making smart choices early, you can launch a high-quality neobank app without overspending. It’s all about building smart, not big.

Neobank App Development Cost in India

The cost of creating a neobank app in India normally ranges between INR 15 Lakhs and 50 Lakhs. The actual cost varies depending on app complexity, design, features, technology stack, and the development team's experience.

A basic software with important functionality like account management, financial transfers, and real-time notifications might cost between INR 12 Lakhs and 15 Lakhs. On the other hand, a more sophisticated software that includes features like expense analytics, multi-currency compatibility, AI-powered chatbots, and third-party API connections might cost INR 40 to 50 Lakhs or more.

Fast approaches, pre-built solutions, and selecting an experienced development team can all help reduce costs. While the investment may appear large, a feature-rich neobank app has enormous potential for scalability and long-term benefits in the thriving fintech sector. 

Conclusion 

The cost of neobank app development in 2026 is likely to remain competitive, particularly in India, as technological developments promote efficiency in the development process. While basic banking apps will likely cost between $18,000 and $25,000, more advanced systems that use AI, blockchain, and personalized financial tools may cost $50,000 to $80,000 or more.

To compete in the fintech sector, firms must invest in apps that deliver flawless user experiences, strong security, and creative features suited to client requirements. Partnering with an experienced development team ensures a cost-effective approach that produces high-quality solutions.
